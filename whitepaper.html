XAUt (Tether Gold) Whitepaper

Introduction: Tether Gold (XAUt) is a blockchain-based digital asset backed by physical gold. Each token represents ownership of one troy ounce of fine gold, held in secure Swiss vaults.

Vision

To bridge traditional value and blockchain through secure digital gold ownership.

Features

  • 1:1 Gold-backed
  • Swiss Vault Storage
  • ERC-20 / TRC-20 Compatible
  • Redeemable and Transparent

Use Cases

  • Digital Gold Trading
  • Wealth Preservation
  • Portfolio Diversification
Note: XAUt is subject to regional availability and KYC policies.
